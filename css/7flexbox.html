  • display:flex==>element bocome flex container and child elements become flex items
  • flex items try to remain inside flex container(size change) till their content allow(control by flex-wrap)
  • flex-derection:colum-reverse==>elements of flex container align vertically bottom to top
  • justify-content:
    1. flex-end/flex-start/flex-center==>all content as it is shift end/start/center to the main axis
    2. space-around==>all free space will be distrubuted equally on both side of every element
    3. space-evenly==>all free space will be distrubuted equally in between and both sides of elements
    4. space-between==>all free space will be distrubuted equally in between the elements
  • flex-wrap:(NOTE=>space generate bw lines on wraping(control by align-content))
    1. nowrap(default)/wrap/wrap-reverse==>elements move to next line except overflowing the flex container
    2. wrap-reverse==>wrap with direction of cross line reversed
  • align-items:center/start/end/flex-start/flex-end/self-start/self-end==>align items along cross axis
  • align-content:start/center/end/space-around/space-evenly/space-between==>alignment of space around and bw content boxes along cross axis
  • align-self:flex-start/end/center=>alignment of specific flex item along cross axis
  • flex-shrink/grow:=>size shrinking/growing factor wrt other flex items when changing size of flex container
